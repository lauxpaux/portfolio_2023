Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Laura, and I am a data scientist and machine learning engineer. My passion for using data to solve complex problems has led me to work on a variety of projects, from natural phenomena in the ionosphere research at NASA to investigating happiness trends around the world.


As a data scientist, I have experience in data analysis, machine learning, statistical modeling, and data visualization. I am proficient in Python and SQL and I have experience working with tools such as pandas, numpy, scikit-learn, matplotlib, seaborn, tensorflow and PostgreSQL.


I enjoy collaborating with cross-functional teams and stakeholders to understand business needs and provide data-driven solutions. I am always looking to learn and stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in the field of data science.


One of my biggest strengths is my ability to communicate complex data insights to a non-technical audience. I believe that data should be accessible to everyone, and I strive to make my findings clear and easy to understand.


When I'm not working, I enjoy cooking, running, reading, and going to shows. I also love to work on Data Chisme, a blog for Latinas in tech or interested in tech that I co-founded with my friend Martha.


Thank you for taking the time to view my portfolio. I am excited about the opportunity to work with a team that shares my passion for using data to drive decision-making and create impactful solutions. If you would like to connect with me, please don't hesitate to reach out!
